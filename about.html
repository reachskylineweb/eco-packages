Corporate Profile

Rooted In High Ethical Values, Safety, & Sourcing Compliance

Bell Ecopack is a division of the prestigious **Bell Group**, a conglomerate with diversified operations in engineering, chemicals, pyrotechnics, and hospitality. Following the group's rich manufacturing legacy established in Tamil Nadu, Bell Ecopack serves as a preferred global partner for premium, biodegradable, and sustainable packaging boxes.

FSC® Certified

Our Chain of Custody certification guarantees that the wood fibers and raw kraft papers are harvested from responsibly managed, renewable forests. We manufacture products to EN 1783 safety parameters.

Manufacturing Hub

Our State-of-the-Art 5-Acre Facility

Located in the heart of Virudhunagar, Tamil Nadu, our facility operates strictly under dust-free clean room protocols, incorporating automated German and Italian folding-gluing machinery.

Ethical Values

We adhere to SEDEX guidelines, ensuring 100% fair wages, safe workspaces, and an inclusive manufacturing community for all our 250+ employees.

Quality Standards

All packaging components undergo BRCGS Standard checks, including thickness testing, fat leak checks, and automated camera line matching.

Safety & Compliance

Products conform to EN 1783 guidelines, employing only FDA-approved food-grade coatings, organic soy ink patterns, and non-toxic starches.
