How We Make It

The Luxury Manufacturing Process

Every package is engineered with absolute precision, starting from sustainably sourced raw materials to final state-of-the-art dispatch quality checks.

01

Raw Material Selection

We source FDA-approved food-safe paperboard and FSC-certified virgin kraft paper. All paper undergoes moisture, thickness, and strength checking.

Sustainable & Safe
02

Structural Packaging Design

Our CAD engineers design layouts, die-lines, and 3D folding systems. We ensure optimal material usage and structural resilience.

Precision Engineering
03

Precision Die Cutting

High-speed automatic platen die-cut machines stamp shapes and score fold lines with millimeter accuracy to guarantee seamless folding later.

Automated Shaping
04

Printing & Branding

Using advanced multi-color offset and eco-friendly water-based flexo inks, we apply rich colors, gold foil accents, and branding patterns.

Eco-Luxury Aesthetics
05

Folding & Assembly

State-of-the-art folder gluers fold, glue, and lock shapes using non-toxic food-grade adhesives at speeds up to 10,000 units per hour.

Automated Gluer Systems
06

Quality Inspection

Inline camera inspection systems analyze every single box for prints alignment, adhesive consistency, and structural stability.

Zero Defect Policy
07

Packaging & Dispatch

Inspected boxes are flat-packed in heavy-duty outer cartons, heat-sealed, palletized, and prepared for global shipping.

Global Delivery Ready
